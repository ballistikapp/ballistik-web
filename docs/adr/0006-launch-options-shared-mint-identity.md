# Launch Options and shared mint identity

Vanity mint intent and Launch Attribution are Ballistik Launch Options, not pump.fun Platform configuration. The shared lifecycle owns mint identity for every Launch via a durable `LaunchPlannedMint` row (vanity pool reserve or fresh key at plan time), attribution apply-at-publish over user-authored description, and the related usage fees; Platforms consume those outcomes. Authoritative plans persist a secret-free envelope (`optionsOutcomes` with always-present `mintPublicKey` / `plannedMintId`, plus `money` and `platformPlan`) rather than burying vanity/attribution inside the pump plan. Execute resolves the mint only through `plannedMintId` (no generate-at-execute). Options fee composition lives above the Platform module; envelope `money` is the only authoritative monetary summary. We rejected leaving these flags in pump `config` because description and mint identity are cross-Platform concerns and the funnel was teaching the wrong model before SPL exists.
